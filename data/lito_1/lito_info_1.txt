The Temptation of St. Anthony
Salvador Dalí
90cm x 119.5cm
The Temptation of Saint Anthony is a painting made by the Spanish painter Salvador Dalí in 1946. The work is painted in oil on canvas, is surreal and measures 90 x 119.5 cm. It is kept in Brussels at the Royal Museums of Fine Arts of Belgium. (Wikipedia, Google Translate)
