The Portrait of Adele Bloch-Bauer I, also known as The Golden Lady or The Golden Lady, is a painting by Gustav Klimt completed in 1907. According to newspaper reports, it was sold for $ 135 million to Ronald Lauder, owner of the Neue Galerie in New York in June 2006, making it the second highest-value painting worldwide. The work has been exhibited in the mentioned gallery since July 2006. (Wikipedia, Google Translate)
