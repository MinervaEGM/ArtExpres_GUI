Lovers
René Magritte
54cm x 73cm
Lovers is a surrealist painting by René Magritte made in Paris in 1928. It is the first work in a series of four variations. In it, the kiss of a couple is represented in the foreground with faces covered by fabrics that hide their identities. It is currently at MoMA in New York, in the Richard S. Zeisler collection.
