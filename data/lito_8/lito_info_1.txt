Café Terrace at Night
Vincent van Gogh
81cm × 65.5cm
Cafe terrace at night (also known as Cafe terrace of the Place du Forum in Arles at night) is a painting by Dutchman Vincent van Gogh made in Arles in September 1888 depicting the ambience of a terrace.
The style of this painting is unique to Van Gogh with warm colors and depth of perspective. This is the first painting in which Vincent used starry backgrounds. Later came Starry Night over the Rhone and Starry Night in 1889. This work falls into the category of paintings by this author that seek to illuminate the night by giving a tint of joy and color to the night landscape.
